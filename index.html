Core Concept & Genre

Title: Project Starfall

Genre: First-Person Shooter (FPS), Tower Defense, Survival, Action-Defense, Base Building

Project Starfall is a single-player, first-person survival tower defense game set in space. The player is a survivor crash-landed on a planet with a damaged spaceship and a critical piece of technology: the False Void Generator. The primary objective is to repair the ship and generator while defending against waves of alien robots and hostile human factions.

Setting & Key Technology

The game takes place in a sector of space featuring the rare "False Void Generator" technology (only 3 known). This generator is the ship's primary power source and FTL drive, making its protection paramount. The player starts on a crash site, needing immediate survival and repair efforts.

Gameplay Loop

  • Initial Survival & Repair: Scavenge resources, perform preliminary repairs on the ship and False Void Generator core.
  • Resource Gathering: Explore planets to mine resources for crafting, building, and upgrades.
  • Base Building & Defense: Establish and fortify a base around the ship and generator with defensive structures (walls, turrets, traps).
  • Ship Repair & Preparation: Continue repairing the main spaceship for interplanetary travel.
  • Wave Defense: Defend against increasingly difficult waves of enemies in a first-person tower defense style.
  • Exploration & Expansion: Travel to other planets, each with unique resources, environments, and challenges.
  • Upgrades: Improve weapons, armor, defenses, spaceship capabilities, and potentially mechs.
  • Combat: First-person engagement with enemies, utilizing defensive structures and vehicles.
  • Fleet Encounters: Occasional large-scale enemy fleet encounters in space.
  • Survival: Manage player health, stamina, and environmental hazards.

Key Features

  • First-Person Perspective for all gameplay.
  • Core Tower Defense mechanics against enemy waves.
  • Survival elements: resource management, crafting.
  • Base Building with modular or freeform construction.
  • Resource Mining from diverse planetary environments.
  • Upgradable systems for player, defenses, ship, and mechs.
  • Player-controlled Spaceships for interplanetary travel.
  • Playable Vehicles including mechs, bikes, and airships.
  • Diverse Enemies: Aliens, robots, hostile human factions with unique classes.
  • Focus on continuous survival with no planned end game.

Target Platform: Single Player, Unity Engine 2022.3.10f1

Enemy Faction Unit Classes (Examples)

Alien Faction:

Drone (Swarming), Scout (Recon), Soldier (Standard), Vanguard (Heavy Armor), Librarian (Support/Debuff), Behemoth (Large Siege), Leviathan (Massive Boss-like).

Robot Faction:

Drone (Utility/Attack), Scout (Fast Recon), Soldier (Combat Bot), Vanguard (Shielded/Heavy Weapons), Librarian (Repair/Buff Bot), Behemoth (Large Platform), Leviathan (Factory/Command Unit).

Hostile Human Faction:

Drone (Armed), Scout (Stealth Sniper), Soldier (Infantry), Vanguard (Heavy Infantry), Librarian (Medic/Commander), Behemoth (Powered Armor/Tank), Leviathan (Large Vehicle/Command Center).
