Core Concept

Game Name: Project Everfield

Genre: Action RPG

Engine: Unity

Platform: Desktop (PC)

Project Everfield is envisioned as an action RPG set in the fantasy world of Everfield. Gameplay focuses on exploration, souls-like combat mechanics (melee and ranged), and a deep magic system involving elemental matchups and dynamic spell interactions. Character progression involves stat allocation and skill development.

World of Everfield

Everfield is a world defined by diverse and magical biomes. Notable regions include:

  • Crystal Oasis Desert: Dark sands, towering mana gems, ancient mage ruins.
  • Blossom Valley: Cherry blossoms, hot springs, waterfalls.
  • Forest of the World Tree: Giant mahogany trees, colossal World Tree at its center.
  • Dead Forest of the World Tree: Charred, desolate version of its living counterpart.
  • Fragmented Lands: Surreal landscape of floating islands from a cataclysmic battle.
  • Flooded Forest: Vast forested wetlands with deep underwater abysses.
  • Volcanic Mountain Range: Rugged peaks with lava flows meeting the sea.
  • Giant Ice Shards: Frozen landscape with towering ice formations and dense forests.
  • World of Bioluminescence: Ethereally glowing flora, fauna, and waterways.
  • Cradle of Lakes: Interconnected crater lakes forming a network of waterways.

A variety of races inhabit Everfield, including Humans, Dragons, Bharons, Angels, Chitarin, Elves, Lyssandra, Homunculus, Beast folk, Flugals, Kitsune, Tenguuru, Mechanoids, Golems, Vampires, Ents, and Dwarfs.

Gameplay Mechanics Overview

Combat System:

Third-person, souls-like combat with stamina management. Melee features stance switching (similar to For Honor) with directional attacks, blocks, and parries. Ranged combat uses an over-the-shoulder view.

Weapon types are categorized: Light, Mid, Heavy, and Ultra Heavy for melee; Bows, Slings, Throwing Weapons, Crossbows, and Firearms for ranged.

Player Stats:

Strength, Finesse, Agility, Endurance, Charisma, Arcane, Mind, Soul. Each stat influences various combat and non-combat attributes (e.g., Melee Prowess, Carry Capacity, Spellcasting Proficiency, Mana Reservoir).

Magic & Spell Crafting:

A core feature is the elemental magic system with strengths/weaknesses (e.g., Fire strong vs. Earth, weak vs. Water). Affinity bonuses enhance elemental specialization. Dynamic spell interactions occur with the environment and other spells (e.g., Fire + Air = Firestorm).

An enhanced spell crafting system allows players to define spell type (Projectile, Beam, AoE), assign energy sources/elements, and add modifiers using crafting materials. Spell power and duration scale with stats and material quality.

Physical Damage Taken = (SUM(max(0, Attack Value - Armor Reduction))) * (1 - Physical Resistance)

Total Damage Taken = Physical Damage Taken + Magical Damage Taken

Other Key Features

  • Player Movement: Includes climbing, swimming, jumping, running, dodging, rolling.
  • Death Mechanics: Penalties like EXP loss, potential skill loss, or even character deletion depending on game mode.
  • Exploration: Hidden paths, secrets, and puzzles.
  • NPC Interactions: Dialogue choices, quests, factions, trading, pickpocketing, recruitment.
  • Crafting/Upgrading: For weapons, armor, and consumables.
  • Boss Mechanics: Unique abilities, phases, and weaknesses.
