Core Concept & Overview

Project Everfield is an Action RPG set in a high-fantasy world. It features souls-like combat, diverse magical biomes, and an intricate spell-crafting system. The primary platform is PC, utilizing the Unity engine.

Game DevWorld BuildingRPG

World of Everfield: Biomes

Explore diverse regions such as the Crystal Oasis Desert, Blossom Valley, the colossal Forest of the World Tree (and its dead counterpart), the surreal Fragmented Lands, Flooded Forests, Volcanic Ranges, Giant Ice Shards, a World of Bioluminescence, and the Cradle of Lakes.

World BuildingEnvironment

Inhabitants: Races & Creatures

Everfield is populated by a multitude of races including Humans, Dragons, Angels, Elves, Kitsune, Mechanoids, Golems, Vampires, and Dwarfs, alongside various unique creatures like Gnomes.

World BuildingCharacter Design

Gameplay Mechanics: Combat & Magic

Features third-person, souls-like combat with stance switching and diverse weapon types. The magic system involves elemental matchups, affinity bonuses, dynamic spell interactions, and a comprehensive spell crafting system based on components, energy types, and modifiers.

Final Spell Power = Base Power * (Scaling Stat Mod * Energy Type Multiplier * Affinity Multiplier * Crafting Bonus)
Game DevCombat DesignMagic System

Player Progression & Other Features

Players can climb, swim, jump, and manage stamina. Death mechanics include EXP loss. Exploration involves secrets and puzzles. NPC interactions offer dialogue, quests, and factions. Crafting allows upgrades for weapons, armor, and consumables.
